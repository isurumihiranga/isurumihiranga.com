Enhancing Machine Learning-Based Techniques for Identifying Fruit Fly Species in the Dry Zone of Sri Lanka

Group Research Project (Ongiong) - Rajarata University of Sri Lanka

  • Improved dataset quality for fruit fly identification using class balancing and augmentation.
  • Optimized feature selection and preprocessing to enhance model performance.
  • Compared ML algorithms for morphological classification of fruit fly species.
  • Developed an ensemble model to boost classification accuracy and robustness.
  • Validated ensemble performance using real-world and benchmark datasets.
